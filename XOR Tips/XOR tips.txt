Xor of even-no-of-set-bits number with odd-no-of-set-bits number :
if we xor two xor-even numbers or two xor-odd numbers (numbers having an odd number of 1s in their binary representations),
we get a xor-even number and,
similarly, if we xor a xor-even number with a xor-odd number, we get a xor-odd number.
